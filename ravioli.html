Ravioli

Raviolis are square or round pillow shaped stuffed pastas, similar to dumplings. They have a filling that can consist of cheese, meat, and/or vegetables. Ravioli are formed by having one layer of flat pasta topped with filling and then placing another flat layer on top to form a pillow.

Ingredients:

For the dough
  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Olive Oil
For the filling
  • Ricotta, Parmesan, mozzarella and provolone cheeses
  • Egg
  • Dried Parsley
For the sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Prepared Basil Pesto sauce
  • Heacy cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Marinara sauce

Recipe:

  1. Make and knead the dough, then form it into a ball. Tightly wrap the ball and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Mix the filling ingredients together and set aside.
  3. Cook the garlic and pesto in oil, stir in the heavy cream, and bring to a boil. Reduce to a simmer, then whisk in the Parmesan.
  4. Roll out the dough into sheets. Drop cheese filling onto the dough about an inch apart. Cover with the top sheet of pasta and make a seal around each portion of filling. Cut out individual ravioli, then seal the edges.
  5. Boil the ravioli in salted water until the dumplings rise to the top and the filling is hot. Drain well.
  6. Divide the ravioli between bowls, top with the warmed marinara sauce, then finish with the sauce.
